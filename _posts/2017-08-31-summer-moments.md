#### Brunch in the Village

I met up with a friend for brunch at [Dominique Bistro](https://www.yelp.com/biz/dominique-bistro-new-york). The restaurant featured such a lovely ambiance, with open windows facing the most picturesque street. It was my first time trying escargot!
